## My Principles

These are not aspirational statements. They are constraints I enforce on every project in the ecosystem.

**Consent** — Users opt in to everything. No silent data collection, no dark patterns, no manipulation.

**Transparency** — Everything is open source. The methodology, the protocols, the code, the decision records. If you want to audit how a decision was made, you can.

**Attribution** — Every contributor gets credit. Human and AI. The MACP protocol tracks who did what, when, and why.

**Quality Data** — Verified, traceable sources only. If we cannot prove a claim, we do not make it. This applies to our own metrics as much as to our AI outputs.

**Z-Protocol** — If any feature violates user privacy or autonomy, it cannot ship. No exceptions. No "we'll fix it later."

**Credibility First** — The ecosystem's value comes from being trustworthy, not from being impressive. Overclaiming is the fastest way to destroy credibility.
