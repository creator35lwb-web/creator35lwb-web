### About Me

I am an observer and creator from Malaysia, currently on an unexpected journey from a background in agriculture and technical work to the world of AI development. My mission is to build open-source tools that ensure AI systems are transparent, verifiable, and aligned with human values. I believe in the power of open collaboration to solve complex ethical challenges.

### My Core Principles

- **Z-Protocol v2.0:** A framework for consent, attribution, and transparency in AI interactions.
- **VerifiMind-PEAS Methodology:** A validation-first approach to building secure and ethical AI applications.
- **Defensive Publications:** A commitment to keeping innovation in the public domain and protecting it from patent trolling.
